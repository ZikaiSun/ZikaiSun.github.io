Biography

I am Zikai Sun, born in November 1998. I majored in Information Engineering (the Honors Class) in South China University of Technology. I am currently a fourth-year undergraduate student.

I am highly fortunate to be advised by Professor Lianwen Jin. Under his advise, I've done some research in deep learning algorithms and have published two papers.

I will continue my research as a Ph.D student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong since september 2019.
